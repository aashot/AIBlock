Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AIBlock. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the AIBlock Chrome Extension.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal data.

AIBlock is designed to be completely private and local. The extension functions entirely within your browser on your local device.

  • No Tracking: We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any other telemetry to track your usage.
  • No External Servers: We do not send your LinkedIn feed data, your browsing history, your IP address, or your custom keyword lists to any external servers.
  • Local Storage: Your custom blocked keywords and extension settings (such as the enable/disable toggle) are saved locally on your device via Chrome's synchronized storage API.

3. Permissions Explained

The AIBlock extension requests the following permissions for specific, limited purposes:

  • storage: Required to save your keyword filter list and toggle settings so they persist between browsing sessions.
  • contextMenus: Required to add a right-click menu option that lets you quickly block a keyword directly from the LinkedIn page.
  • https://www.linkedin.com/*: Required so the extension's content script can read the text on your LinkedIn feed to determine if a post should be hidden based on your keywords.

4. Third-Party Links

Our extension acts strictly on LinkedIn.com. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by LinkedIn Corporation or Microsoft.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
